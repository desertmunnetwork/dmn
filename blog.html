GMA MUN

Aarav Mamtani / 27 November 2024 / Model United Nations

GMAMUN?

It was a wonderful experience at GMAMUN. If I was new to MUN and I had to pick an MUN to go to, GMAMUN would be the first pick. At a conference with 800+ delegates, it was truly an enriching experience and I got exposed to people of various diverse cultures.

With stellar debates, ceremonies and a social night that I am never forgetting. GMAMUN truly made my 25th, 26th, and 27th October weekend amazing!

Who made this happen?

The brilliant secretariat of GMAMUN made this conference happen in under 28 days. As noted from Manas Kankarej's speech, they had an amazing team with the Head of Chairs: Nikhil being a background Secretary General. This conference wouldn't have happened without these brilliant young men who have indefinetely shaped the UAE MUN demographic for future MUNs

Their conference had notable speakers, one being a LITERAL Delegate of India in the UNHRC, and he was also the Director General of GMAMUN during his school years. This conference will truly go down in history!

Futuristic Crisis Committee

The FCC or the Futuristic Crisis Committee was one of the most notable committees at GMAMUN where they had an Avengers Crisis, Mr. Charles giving a speech as THANOS, OMA (ft. FCC) crisis. This committee was a crisis. Literally.

The other committees were amazing, but in my heart the FCC was one of the best committees I have ever Co-Chaired and it will always have a special place in my heart. Without the Secretary General: Manas Kankarej, this wouldn't have happened. He truly made an impact on all 800+ people present at GMAMUN during that weekend. Long Live GMAMUN or as Heetaarth Udeshi likes to say: "Bharat Mata Ki Jai!"
